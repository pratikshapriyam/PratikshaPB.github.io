Welcome to my webpage. I am a 5th-year PhD student at IISER Pune, working under the supervision of Dr. Neena Joseph Mani. My research focuses on the large-scale atmospheric circulation and its dynamics.

Moist convection plays a crucial role in shaping the strength and variability of the Hadley circulation. Given that tropical moist convection operates across multiple spatial and temporal scales, a detailed investigation into the influence of different tropical eddies on regional Hadley cell dynamics is essential.
