About Me

Welcome to my webpage! I am a 5th-year PhD student at IISER Pune, working under the supervision of Dr. Neena Joseph Mani. My PhD research primarily involves enhancing our understanding of the dynamics of regional Hadley Cells across different geographical domains. Particularly I investigate the influence of different large scale climate modes and various tropical, extratropical factors towards their variations.
