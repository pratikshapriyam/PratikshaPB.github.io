About Me

I am a 5th-year PhD student in the Earth and Climate Science department at IISER Pune, working under the supervision of Dr. Neena Joseph Mani. My PhD research focuses on understanding the variability of regional Hadley circulations and the factors modulating these variations, including scale interactions and tropical-extratropical influences.

To know more about my research interests, click here.

I am originally from Assam, India and have previously completed Integrated M.Sc in Physics from Dibrugarh University, Assam in 2019.
