Key research highlights

1. Regional variations in Hadley circulation

Most of the studies till date have concentrated on studying the variability and trends in the strength and extent of the global mean Hadley Circulation (HC), which may not often reflect the region-to-region differences in the HC. There are several unanswered questions pertaining to the characteristics of the various regional HCs. In this study we provide a comprehensive analysis of the regional HCs across six distinct geographic domains.

Reference: AGU 2023 Abstract

2. Influence of Tropical Waves

Tropical waves, such as the Mixed Rossby-Gravity (MRG) waves, can impact the regional Hadley circulation. I investigate how these waves contribute to seasonal and interannual variability.

Reference: JGR: Atmospheres

Figure 2: Propagation of MRG waves in the tropics

3. Role of Moist Convection

Moist convection plays a crucial role in modulating large-scale atmospheric circulations. I study the mechanisms through which convective processes shape the Hadley cell dynamics.
